ChronoCase – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/07/25

ChronoCase (“we,” “our,” or “us”) built Chrono Case as a commercial app available via the Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

ChronoCase does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not:

Require you to create an account.
Collect your name, email address, or contact information.
Use analytics or tracking SDKs to monitor your usage across other apps or services.
2. In-App Purchases

The app offers in-app purchases of additional game cases. These transactions are processed securely via Apple’s App Store and we do not access or store your payment details.

3. Location Data

EChronoCase uses your device’s location to enable gameplay at historical sites. Location information is:

Used only on your device for determining whether you are near the correct location to unlock content.
Not collected, stored, or transmitted by us.
You can disable location permissions in your device settings. However, doing so may limit functionality.

4. Camera Access

If you choose to use the selfie feature at the end of a game, the app may access your device’s camera. Images you take are:

Saved only to your device.
Not transmitted to us or any third party.
5. Children’s Privacy

ChronoCase does not target or knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can remove it.
